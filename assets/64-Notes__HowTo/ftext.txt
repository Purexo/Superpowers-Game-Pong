# Rules
To win you should reach 500 score
each ball bounce on player are save in a var tmpScore
when you goal the adverse camp, you obtain tmpScore

# Controls
P1 (on right) :
↑ ↓ → ← : to control your player

P2 (on left) :
WASD or ZQSD : to control your player

Miscelanous :
X                 : to reset game
Escape            : to stop game
NUMPAD9 - NUMPAD3 : increase Master  volume
NUMPAD8 - NUMPAD2 : increase Music   volume
NUMPAD7 - NUMPAD1 : increase SoundFX volume
